Ishan Pranav Kumar Pandey Kishan

(born 18 July 1998) is an Indian cricketer from Bihar. He made his international debut for the India cricket team in March 2021.[1][2] In December 2015 he was named as the captain of India's squad for the 2016 Under-19 Cricket World Cup.[3] Kishan is a left-handed batsman and wicket-keeper. In the players auction for the 2018 Indian Premier League Kishan was bought by Mumbai Indians for Rs. 6.2 Crores.
